01 — The Product

What it is

M-E CEO stands for Marketing is Everything CEO — rooted in Philip Kotler's philosophy that marketing is not a department, it is the business. The aspiration is that by Day 30, the client thinks and operates as a Marketing-first CEO.

The Promise
"In 30 days, you go from a side hustle you can't explain to a real business you know how to run — and you make your first KSh 5,000 from complete strangers online."
Format
Done-With-You
Not a video library. Not a self-paced course. Karl is actively present — daily in Week 1, three times a week in Weeks 2–4. The client never figures it out alone.
Operating Environment
Notion Workspace
Every session, assignment, and output lives in Notion. The client keeps the workspace permanently after Day 30 — it becomes their business operating system.
Price
KSh 4,500
One price. No hidden tiers. Covered by a full money-back guarantee if the client completes every session and does not make their first KSh 5,000 from strangers by Day 30.
Duration
30 Days
Four structured weeks with a clear theme each — Clarity, Structure, Customers, Systems. Short enough to commit to. Long enough to produce something real.
What the client has on Day 30
A named, branded business identity
A written offer — what they sell, who for, what it costs
A price they say without apologising
A payment policy clients have signed
An active Instagram and TikTok presence
A Notion workspace running their business
Their first KSh 5,000 from strangers online
The identity: I finally know what I'm doing
02 — The Avatar

Who it's for

The Side Hustle Hobbyist. Not the pure beginner with no business. Not the established SME owner. The person in between — who has been running something for 3–12 months, has made real money, but it still feels like a hobby.

"I want to stop being a hobbyist and become an actual entrepreneur and make money even for future use."
— Real words from Karl's first client, before buying
Who they are
  • Running one or more side hustles — sometimes overlapping
  • 3–12 months in, has made real money
  • Has not separated business money from personal
  • Only sells to people they already know
  • Late-paying clients are a recurring problem
  • Inconsistent revenue — no system behind it
  • Burns energy, sees little structural growth
Their language
  • "I'm the issue"
  • "Activity vs productivity"
  • "I can only sell to people I know"
  • "My clients always pay late"
  • "I make money but don't know where it goes"
  • "I keep starting and stopping"
  • "This still doesn't feel like a real business"
Why Karl can serve this person
Karl lost KSh 78,000 to a network marketing venture that promised to teach him business, handed him sales tutorials, and left him alone. The M-E CEO System is the exact opposite of that experience — structured, hand-held, system-first. Karl is not serving a stranger. He is serving a younger version of himself. That is not marketing. That is the reason the product exists.
